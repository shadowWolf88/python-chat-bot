| Phase | Timeline | Tasks |

## ðŸ’¡ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does REC approval take?**  
A: Typically 4-8 weeks. See [Research Ethics Guide](./Research-Ethics-Guide.md) for timeline.

**Q: Can I customize the measurement scales?**  
A: Yes, but you must validate any changes. See [Measurement Scales](./Measurement-Scales.md).

**Q: How do I export participant data for analysis?**  
A: See [Data Collection Guide](./Data-Collection-Guide.md) - one-click export in CSV/SPSS/R formats.

**Q: What if a participant is at high risk of suicide?**  
A: System alerts clinician immediately. See [Safety Monitoring Guide](./Safety-Monitoring-Guide.md).

**Q: Can I publish my findings?**  
A: Yes! See [Publication Guide](./Publication-Guide.md) for authorship, pre-registration, and open science guidance.

**Q: What happens to data after the trial?**  
A: See [Data Collection Guide](./Data-Collection-Guide.md) - retention period configurable, secure deletion on schedule.
