HEALING SPACE
Pilot Study — Student Clinicians & Student Volunteers
------------------------------------------------------------

We are recruiting student clinicians and student volunteers for a supervised digital mental health pilot.

Who we need:
- Student clinicians (psychology/medicine trainees) — supervised practice in remote check-ins.
- Student volunteers (any discipline) — help evaluate mood tracking and clinician sessions.

Why join?
- Clinicians: supervised, practical experience; reflective learning; CV-relevant clinical exposure.
- Participants: free, confidential check-ins, mood tracking, and contribution to improving student-led care.

Time commitment:
- Clinicians: 1× 90-minute training + up to 6 supervised sessions across 4 weeks.
- Patients: brief registration, daily/weekly mood logs, optional 1–6 sessions across 4 weeks.

Incentives:
- Certificate of participation, course credit options (where available), and refreshments for training sessions.
