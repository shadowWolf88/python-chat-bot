- Private by default, public by choice

- Content warnings standard (not optional)

- Accessible to users with disabilities (vision, hearing, motor)
- Timezone awareness (not US-centric)

### 5. Transparency & Trust
- Moderation decisions explained
- Algorithm design visible (no black boxes)
- Conflicts of interest disclosed
- Regular trust reports + audits

### 6. Anti-Exploitation
- No data monetization without consent
- No addiction mechanics (variable rewards)
- Users own their content
- No shadow banning (transparent removal)
