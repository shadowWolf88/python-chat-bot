Adaptive Intelligence

An AI That Truly Gets to Know You

Most mental health apps give everyone the same experience. Healing Space does the opposite — an AI that listens deeply, builds understanding over time, and grows smarter alongside your clinician.

👂

Listens Without Judgement

The AI engages with you the way a trusted confidant might — openly, patiently, without agenda. It picks up on your language, your emotional patterns, and the things that matter to you most, creating a space where you can speak freely at any hour of the day.

🧠

Remembers What Matters

Unlike a one-size-fits-all chatbot, our AI builds a longitudinal picture of your journey. It knows your history, your triggers, your breakthroughs, and your setbacks — and uses that accumulated context every single time you return, so you never have to start from scratch.

🔄

Grows With Your Clinician

Your clinician reviews, guides, and actively shapes the AI's approach to your care. Each clinical insight refines how the AI supports you — a continuous feedback loop where human expertise and machine intelligence strengthen each other over time.

Care that compounds. The more you engage, the better the AI understands you. The more your clinician contributes, the smarter the system becomes. Healing Space isn't just a tool — it's a clinical relationship that deepens with every conversation, building a version of support that is uniquely, precisely yours.

Platform Features

Everything You Need for Your Wellbeing

Six powerful tools, thoughtfully designed — all in one secure, clinician-supervised environment. Click any card to learn more.

🧠

AI Mental Health Companion

24/7 AI-powered conversations, CBT exercises, and psychoeducation, all clinician-supervised.

  • Available around the clock, 365 days a year
  • Grounded in evidence-based CBT and ACT frameworks
  • Every conversation reviewable by your clinician
  • Psychoeducation modules on anxiety, depression, sleep & stress
📊

Mood & Progress Tracking

Log your mood daily, visualise trends, and share insights directly with your clinician.

  • Log mood on a 1–10 scale with emotion tags
  • Visual trend graphs spanning days, weeks, and months
  • Clinician-shareable PDF progress reports
  • Automated weekly summaries sent to your care team
📓

CBT Workbooks & Journaling

Guided cognitive behavioural therapy tools: thought records, exposure hierarchies, values exercises, and more.

  • Thought Records using the ABC cognitive model
  • Behavioural Activation scheduling tools
  • Exposure Hierarchy builder for anxiety management
  • Core Belief and Values clarification exercises
🎯

Goal Setting & Milestones

Set meaningful recovery goals, track milestones, and celebrate progress with your care team.

  • SMART goal framework with clinician oversight
  • Visual milestone timeline and progress celebrations
  • Goals visible to both patient and clinician
  • Weekly check-in prompts to maintain momentum
💬

Secure Clinician Messaging

Direct, GDPR-compliant messaging between patients and their assigned clinician.

  • Encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) & at rest, UK GDPR compliant
  • Full message history retained and auditable
  • Read receipts and timestamps on all messages
  • File and document sharing supported
🔒

Safe, Private, Confidential

Fully encrypted data, NHS-aligned safeguarding, and full data control — always yours.

  • Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-128 CBC)
  • Your data is never sold to third parties — ever
  • Full Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) support
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten) on request

Three Steps to Better Wellbeing

Getting started is simple, safe, and clinician-guided from day one. Click each step to find out more.

🏥

Request Access

Your clinician or care team refers you to the platform. You'll receive a secure registration link directly to your email.

Your GP, psychiatrist, psychologist, or mental health keyworker refers you directly. You'll receive a personalised, time-limited registration link by email. Setup takes under five minutes — no forms, no waiting rooms, no waiting lists.
🤝

Meet Your Clinician

Complete your onboarding, get matched with your clinician, and set your wellbeing goals together in a safe environment.

Access your tools anytime. Your clinician monitors your progress and adjusts your care plan as you grow.

Work through CBT tools at your own speed, log your mood daily, and message your clinician whenever you need. Your clinician reviews your progress data regularly and adjusts your care plan as you grow — support is always calibrated to where you are right now.
For Clinicians

Built for Clinicians Who Care

A complete digital care environment — patient dashboards, session notes, risk monitoring, secure messaging, and AI-assisted insights. Click each feature to learn more.

One unified view of every patient on your caseload. Sort and filter by risk level, last mood entry, outstanding homework, or session recency. Real-time status badges keep you informed at a glance — nothing falls through the cracks.
Colour-coded risk alerts update automatically as patients complete daily check-ins and AI conversations. Set custom thresholds and receive immediate in-platform notifications. View a 30-day mood trajectory for every patient on a single timeline.
Before each session, receive an AI-structured summary of your patient's recent interactions — key themes explored, cognitive distortions identified, homework engagement, and suggested clinical focus areas. More informed sessions, less prep time.
Direct, encrypted messaging with each patient. Every message is stored securely under UK GDPR with full audit trails, read receipts, and archiving built in. No third-party access. No data sold. Ever.
Assign tailored exercises directly from your dashboard — thought records, behavioural activation logs, exposure hierarchies, values clarification, and core belief worksheets. Review completed work with inline clinical notes before your next session.
Immediate alerts — in-platform and by email — when a patient's mood data, AI responses, or safety screeners cross a risk threshold. Includes clear crisis pathway guidance, escalation contacts, and one-click tools to initiate urgent outreach.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Healing Space UK is available to adults (18+) in the UK who are under the care of a registered clinician — including GPs, psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, and CBT therapists. Access is via clinical referral only. It is not a crisis or emergency service. If you are in crisis, please contact NHS 111 or the Samaritans on 116 123.
Access pricing is determined by your referring clinician or practice. Some NHS-adjacent partnerships may offer subsidised or fully funded access. Contact your clinical team or reach out to us directly to learn more about pricing and access options for your organisation.
All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-128 CBC (Fernet). We are fully compliant with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. We never sell your data to third parties, and we support full Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) and the right to erasure at any time.
Currently, access requires a clinical referral. This ensures you receive the safest and most effective care — our AI tools are designed to complement clinical oversight, not replace it. We are exploring self-referral pathways and will update this page when available.
The platform works on any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge — on desktop, tablet, or mobile phone. No app download is required. The interface is fully responsive and meets WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards.
The AI companion is grounded in clinically-validated CBT and ACT frameworks, and all conversations are stored and reviewable by your assigned clinician. It cannot prescribe medication, make diagnoses, or replace therapeutic care. It is a supportive tool designed for use between sessions, always under clinical supervision.
Clinicians can request practice access by contacting us via email at r.martin@healing-space.org.uk. Once your practice is onboarded, you can issue secure registration links to patients directly from your clinician dashboard. Setup for your practice typically takes less than 24 hours.
