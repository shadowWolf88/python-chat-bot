Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Healing Space UK is available to adults (18+) in the UK who are under the care of a registered clinician ‚Äî including GPs, psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, and CBT therapists. Access is via clinical referral only. It is not a crisis or emergency service. If you are in crisis, please contact NHS 111 or the Samaritans on 116 123.
Access pricing is determined by your referring clinician or practice. Some NHS-adjacent partnerships may offer subsidised or fully funded access. Contact your clinical team or reach out to us directly to learn more about pricing and access options for your organisation.
All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256. We are fully compliant with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. We never sell your data to third parties, and we support full Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) and the right to erasure at any time.
Currently, access requires a clinical referral. This ensures you receive the safest and most effective care ‚Äî our AI tools are designed to complement clinical oversight, not replace it. We are exploring self-referral pathways and will update this page when available.
The platform works on any modern browser ‚Äî Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge ‚Äî on desktop, tablet, or mobile phone. No app download is required. The interface is fully responsive and meets WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards.
The AI companion is grounded in clinically-validated CBT and ACT frameworks, and all conversations are stored and reviewable by your assigned clinician. It cannot prescribe medication, make diagnoses, or replace therapeutic care. It is a supportive tool designed for use between sessions, always under clinical supervision.
Clinicians can request practice access by contacting us via email at r.martin@healing-space.org.uk. Once your practice is onboarded, you can issue secure registration links to patients directly from your clinician dashboard. Setup for your practice typically takes less than 24 hours.
