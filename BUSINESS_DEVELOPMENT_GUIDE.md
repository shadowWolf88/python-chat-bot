**Positioning:** Healing Space is a **proprietary, clinical-grade mental health platform** combining:
- AI-powered therapeutic conversations (Groq LLM)
- Columbia-C-SSRS suicide risk assessment (validated)
- CBT toolkit + mood tracking
- Clinician oversight & patient safety monitoring
- GDPR/HIPAA-ready infrastructure

**Strategy: Dynamic Pilot Pricing** (not open-source, not free forever)

**Revenue Model:**
1. **Organizations with budgets** (Universities, NHS) → Small paid pilots (£500-2k) = immediate revenue
2. **Price-sensitive orgs** (Therapy practices, charities) → Free 2-week trials → Paid upgrades (£200-500/month)
3. **Win strategy:** Every pilot = case study + testimonial for next sale

**Expected 6-month outcome:**
- 3-5 paying customers
- £15k-30k in pilot fees revenue
- 5-10 case studies for enterprise sales
- Path to £50k+ NHS contracts by year-end

## **PART 10: MESSAGING & TONE**

### **Key Positioning (NON-negotiable)**

- "Clinical-grade" (not wellness, not consumer app)
- "Therapist-controlled" (you're not replacing anyone)
- "Proprietary" (not free or open-source)
- "Early pilot partners" (selective, not for everyone)
- "Outcomes-focused" (you care about real impact, not vanity metrics)

### **What to Say**

**DO:**
- "We reduce wait times"
- "We catch suicide risk early"
- "Your clinicians stay in control"
- "This saves you 10+ hours/week"
- "We're clinical-grade, not a wellness app"

**DON'T:**
- "It's free!" (implies low quality)
- "Open-source" (implies you don't own it)
- "Replaces therapists" (scary, untrue)
- "Works for everyone" (not credible)
- "FDA approved" (you're not, don't claim it)

## **PART 11: RED FLAGS & WALK-AWAYS**

**Don't pursue if:**
- They want it 100% free with no commitment
- They expect you to white-label for free "to prove concept"
- They demand equity stake without serious funding
- They want "free trial indefinitely"
- They're non-clinical (pure wellness coaching)
- They say "We'll pay if it works" (classic tire-kicker)
  - Counter: "Let's do a small paid pilot—proves you're serious, I deliver real support"
