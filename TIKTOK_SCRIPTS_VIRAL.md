## **PRODUCTION TIPS FOR TIKTOK**

### **Filming (Use iPhone vertical, obviously)**

**Lighting:**
- Natural light is perfect
- Avoid harsh shadows on face
- Ring light if indoors (cheap on Amazon)

**Audio:**
- Use trending TikTok sounds (search "trending audio")
- Voiceover in quiet room (bedroom works)
- Match audio beats to your cuts (sync = more views)

**Editing (Use CapCut — free, built for TikTok):**
- Fast cuts (0.5-1 sec per shot) = engagement
- Text overlays (use trending fonts)
- Transitions (keep it simple)
- Color filters (slight desaturation then bright = trendy)

### **Trending Sounds to Use**

Find these in TikTok audio library:

1. **"Oh No"** — for shock/revelation moments
2. **"Emotional Piano"** — for serious/vulnerable content
3. **"Uplifting String"** — for inspiring outcomes
4. **"Story Time"** — for narrative-driven content
5. **"Urgent Beats"** — for crisis/tension moments

## **POSTING STRATEGY**

**Best times:** 
- 6-9 AM (morning scroll)
- 12-1 PM (lunch break)
- 7-9 PM (evening wind-down)
- 10-11 PM (late-night therapy seekers)

**Post cadence:**
- 3-5 videos per week (consistency matters)
- Respond to comments in first hour (algorithm boost)
- Go live 1x per week (even 10 min = huge engagement)

**What works on TikTok:**
- ✅ Emotional honesty (vulnerability = engagement)
- ✅ Fast pacing (slow = swipe)
- ✅ Clear value (show the benefit immediately)
- ✅ Trending sounds (algorithm favors them)
- ✅ Text overlays (accessibility + searchability)
- ✅ Personal story (relatability > polish)

**What sucks on TikTok:**
- ❌ Long intro (you have 2 seconds)
- ❌ Watermarks from other apps (TikTok hates it)
- ❌ Grainy video (phone quality is fine, but not potato quality)
- ❌ No captions/text (reduces accessibility + reach)
- ❌ Talking directly at camera for 60 sec (boring)
