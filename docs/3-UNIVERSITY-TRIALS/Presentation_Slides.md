# Slide 2 — Problem

- Many students face mental health challenges but access to supervised clinical practice and evaluation tools is limited.
- Digital tools can augment training and reach, but need safety and clinician workflows.

---

# Slide 3 — Solution

- Healing Space: clinician-facing UI + AI-assisted session support, mood tracking, and FHIR export for clinical interoperability.
- Designed for safe supervised student clinician trials.

---

# Slide 4 — Trial Design

- 10 clinicians, 30 patients, 4-week pilot
- Measures: feasibility, acceptability, safety alerts
- Data: anonymized for research with consent

---

# Slide 5 — Clinician Flow

- Training (90m) → onboarding → run sessions → mark attendance → post-session evals

---

# Slide 6 — Patient Flow

- Sign consent → mood logging → optional clinician sessions → post-trial survey
