Measurement Scales

PHQ-9 (depression)
- 9-item scale; scoring and interpretation provided. Use as baseline and at follow-ups.

GAD-7 (anxiety)
- 7-item scale; scoring and interpretation provided.

Administration
- Ask patients to complete at baseline and weekly during the 4-week pilot.
- Store responses in `clinical_scales` table with `entry_timestamp`.
