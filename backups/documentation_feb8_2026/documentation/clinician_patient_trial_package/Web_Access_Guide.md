Web Access Guide — Non-technical (Clinicians & Patients)

Purpose
- Quick instructions for clinicians and participants who will only use the hosted website. No installation or developer work required.

Access
- URL: Your study admin will provide the site address (example: https://your-app.up.railway.app).
- Accounts: The study team will either send you an invite link or create accounts for you. Use the username/password provided.

Clinician (non-technical) — Quick steps
1. Open the provided URL in a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox).
2. Click "Log in" and enter the credentials given by the study admin.
3. Use the top menu to search patients or open your patient list.
4. Open a patient record to view mood logs, PHQ-9/GAD-7 results, and upcoming appointments.
5. During or after a supervised session, use the attendance buttons in the patient modal to mark `attended`, `no_show`, or `cancelled`.
6. If you see a safety alert, follow the safety escalation instructions in the clinician guide and contact your supervisor immediately.

Patient (non-technical) — Quick steps
1. Open the provided URL in a modern browser.
2. Click the account link in your invitation email or use sign-up if instructed.
3. On the dashboard you can:
   - Log mood entries via the "Mood" tab.
   - View scheduled appointments in "Appointments".
   - Chat with the AI assistant using the provided chat box (optional).
4. If you need help, contact the study helpline provided in the consent and support documents.

Notes for everyone
- No local installation required — everything runs in your browser.
- For any access problems (login, missing appointment), contact the study IT support listed in `Support_Contacts.md`.
- Developer-only instructions (installing dependencies, running locally) are in `Quickstart_Local_Testing.md` and are not needed for participants.
