

Healing Space UK – Project Introduction

Empowering Clinicians, Transforming Patient Care
Healing Space UK is a next-generation digital mental health platform designed to revolutionize the way clinicians and therapists support their patients. By combining advanced artificial intelligence, secure data management, and a suite of evidence-based clinical tools, Healing Space UK streamlines workflows, enhances patient engagement, and delivers measurable improvements in mental health outcomes.

What is Healing Space UK?
Healing Space UK is a dual-mode (web and desktop) application that provides a comprehensive mental health companion for both patients and clinicians. It offers a secure and user-friendly environment in which patients can access therapeutic tools, track their wellbeing, and communicate with their care team. Clinicians benefit from automation, data-driven insights, and reduced administrative burden.

Key Benefits for Clinicians and Therapists
• Reduced Workload
Automates routine tasks such as appointment scheduling, mood tracking, and data collection, freeing valuable clinician time.
• AI-Powered Summaries
Instantly generates clinical summaries and risk assessments, allowing clinicians to focus on patient care rather than paperwork.
• Comprehensive Patient Insights
Visual dashboards and trend analysis provide a clear overview of patient progress, supporting informed and effective interventions.
• Secure Messaging and Alerts
Built-in communication tools and crisis detection ensure patients are supported and clinicians are notified of urgent needs.
• GDPR-Compliant Data Management
All patient data is encrypted and managed with full consent controls, supporting ethical and compliant practice.

How Healing Space UK Helps Patients
• 24/7 Support
Patients can access AI-guided therapy, mood tracking, and self-help tools at any time.
• Personalised Wellbeing Tools
Includes gratitude journals, CBT exercises, habit trackers, and a virtual pet for motivation and engagement.
• Crisis Resources
Immediate access to safety plans and crisis contacts, with AI monitoring for high-risk situations.
• Empowerment and Engagement
Encourages patients to take an active role in their care, building resilience and self-awareness.

How Healing Space UK Works (Plain Language Overview)
    1. Easy Onboarding
Patients and clinicians register securely. Clinicians can invite patients and manage caseloads from a central dashboard.
    2. AI Therapy and Chat Support
Patients can chat with an AI therapist between sessions. The AI provides evidence-based guidance and flags potential crisis indicators.
With patient consent, the AI can reference therapist notes and session summaries, ensuring conversations remain continuous and context-aware. This allows therapy goals to be reinforced and support to be tailored to each patient’s unique journey.
    3. Automated Data Collection
The system collects mood, sleep, habits, and clinical assessment data and presents it in clear charts for clinician review.
    4. Appointments and Messaging
Built-in scheduling and secure messaging reduce reliance on phone calls and emails. This is particularly beneficial for patients who experience social anxiety or communication difficulties.
    5. Clinical Summaries and Alerts
Automated summaries and risk alerts reduce administrative workload and improve response times.
    6. GDPR and Security Compliance
All data is encrypted and controlled by patient consent. Healing Space UK is rated to NHS Digital standards and compliant with GDPR and ISO 27001.
Security is regularly audited and certified by independent third parties. Data export and anonymisation tools are included for research and regulatory compliance.

Full Capabilities Overview
• AI Therapy Chatbot (Groq LLM) with empathetic, evidence-based support and crisis detection
• Mood and habit tracking (sleep, hydration, exercise, medication)
• CBT and self-help tools, including guided exercises and journals
• Virtual pet gamification for motivation and engagement
• Clinical assessments such as PHQ-9 and GAD-7 with automated scoring
• Appointment scheduling and automated reminders
• Secure encrypted messaging between patients and clinicians
• Optional moderated peer community support
• Crisis and safety planning tools
• GDPR-compliant data export and deletion
• Full audit and activity logging
• Industry-leading security with end-to-end encryption and multi-factor authentication
• NHS Digital and ISO 27001 compliant infrastructure

With Healing Space UK, clinicians can:
